EMERGENCY CONTACT

See below the steps to take in an emergency and the resources Texas A&M University offers.


A) If you encounter an emergency, call 911.


B) If you encounter a student exhibiting concerning behavior, but it is not an emergency, you can file a report on the Tell Somebody website. Reports can be filed anonymously if you wish.


C) The Tell Somebody website contains information about other on-campus numbers you can call if you have more specific concerns.


D) University Health Services (UHS) provides a wide range of services from crisis to routine. You can download the TELUS Health Student Support app at the UHS website and access professional, confidential counseling 24/7 by phone or chat in multiple languages. This is not an avenue for you to “report” someone else, but you may wish to encourage other students to take advantage of the services that UHS provides.


E) UHS also offers suicide prevention and awareness information and training opportunities.
