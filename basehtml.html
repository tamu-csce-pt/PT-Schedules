Notice:

In-person peer teacher office hours are held in Peer Teacher Central (PTC) in PETR 127 from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. On Sundays, office hours and the Week-In-Review sessions are held from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. via the Discord server. The schedule for the Week-In-Review sessions can be found on the Week-In-Review Page

 To access the PTC Discord:

  1. Watch a tutorial on how to use/download Discord (optional).

  2. Create an account with Discord (there is no requirement to use your university email).
